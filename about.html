Our Story

Where We Started

Leven6 was born from a simple observation: incredible local businesses were struggling to stand out in an increasingly digital world. We saw restaurants with amazing food, boutiques with unique products, and services with exceptional quality all failing to reach their full potential.

We knew there had to be a better way. A way that combined strategic thinking with creative execution. A way that understood both the art and science of modern marketing. That's why we created Leven6.

Today, we're proud to partner with ambitious brands across Sri Lanka, helping them build presence, create impact, and grow smarter.
