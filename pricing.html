Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle, and we'll work with you to ensure a smooth transition.

What's included in the setup process?

Every new client receives a comprehensive onboarding that includes brand analysis, audience research, competitor review, and strategic planning. We'll develop your initial content calendar and brand guidelines to ensure we hit the ground running.

How long is the contract?

We offer flexible month-to-month contracts with no long-term commitment required. However, we recommend at least 3 months to see meaningful results from your marketing efforts.

Do you work with businesses outside Colombo?

Yes! While we're based in Colombo, we work with brands across Sri Lanka. Most of our communication happens virtually, and we can coordinate in-person sessions when needed.

What platforms do you manage?

We primarily focus on Instagram and Facebook, as these platforms deliver the best results for restaurants and local brands. We can also manage other platforms as add-ons based on your specific needs.
